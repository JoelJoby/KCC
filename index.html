About KCC24

Welcome to the Kerala Campus Conference 2024!

Since its inception, the Kerala Campus Conference (KCC) has served as a dynamic gathering point for students and youth across Kerala, offering a vibrant platform for spiritual growth, fellowship, and intellectual engagement. Organized by Jesus Youth, an international Catholic movement with a charismatic spirituality, KCC24 continues a long-standing tradition of fostering faith, character, and friendships in a joyful and sacred environment.
